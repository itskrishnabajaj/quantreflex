🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to QuantReflex

QuantReflex is a precision training platform for mental math and quantitative aptitude. Build speed and accuracy through short, focused daily sessions.

Built for:

  • CAT / GMAT / MBA CET aspirants: sharpen QA section speed
  • Placement preparation: ace aptitude rounds in campus drives
  • School students (Class 6–12): master mental math for boards and classwork
  • NTSE / Olympiad learners: build competitive math reflexes
  • General aptitude learners: anyone who wants faster, more accurate calculations

Here's how to get going in 60 seconds:

  • Sign in or create your account
  • Complete the quick onboarding warmup
  • Pick a practice mode from the Practice tab
  • Answer with the custom numpad: no keyboard distractions
  • Review your results and mistakes after each session
  • Check your progress on the Analytics tab
  • Set a daily goal and turn on reminders in Settings

💡 Use the Daily Warmup on the Home tab every morning to start your streak and ease into the day.

🎯 Practice Modes

⚡ Quick Drill

5 questions, no timer. The fastest way to start sharp — use it every morning to build a daily habit before you dive deeper.

🧠 Reflex Drill

10 questions, 15 seconds each. Builds the muscle memory you need to solve under exam pressure — where every second counts.

⏱ Timed Test

10 questions in 3 minutes. The closest simulation of a real QA section — train your pacing so exam day feels familiar.

🎯 Focus Training

10 questions, one category. Drill weak topics — Percentages, TSD, Profit & Loss — until they stop costing you marks.

🔓 Custom Training

Pick any mix of categories, set count (1–100), and add a timer. Build sessions around your exact syllabus — not a preset someone else designed.

💡 Combine 2–3 weak categories in one session for high-density revision.

🔓 Review Mistakes

Retry every question you got wrong — they stay in your pool until you solve them correctly. The fastest path to recovering marks you're already losing.

💡 Finish every session with a Review Mistakes round. Mistakes not revisited are marks not recovered.

📖 Learn Tab

What's in the Learn Vault

  • Fraction → Percentage: conversion table to memorize cold — saves 3–5 seconds per question
  • Multiplication Tables: quick-reference from 1 to 30
  • Quant Formulas: Profit & Loss, Ratios, Averages, TSD, Time & Work, and more — in one place
  • Shortcut Tricks: mental math techniques that cut your solving time down
  • 🔓 Custom Formulas: add your own notes and formulas — build a personal cheat sheet

Power Features

  • Search: jump directly to any formula without scrolling
  • Jump Navigation: tap section buttons to reach any topic group instantly
  • Bookmarks: star formulas to keep your most-used ones a tap away
  • 🔓 Custom Topics: create your own formula cards and organize your study notes your way
  • 🔓 Full-Screen Tables: triple-tap any table to open it distraction-free — great for memorization sprints

📊 Reading Your Analytics

What Each Metric Means

  • Accuracy %: your correct-answer rate. Get this to 80%+ before you start prioritizing speed.
  • Avg Response Time: your average seconds per question. Below 10s is solid; below 7s is exam-ready.
  • Current Streak: days in a row with at least one session. Protect it — consistency is what compounds over time.
  • Weakest Category: your lowest-accuracy topic. Open Focus Training on it today.
  • 🔓 Category Breakdown: see exactly which topics are costing you marks — and by how much.
  • 🔓 Performance Insights: spot trends in your training before they become exam-day problems.
  • Daily Goal Progress: how close you are to today's target. Hit it every single day.

💡 Check your weakest category every week. If it's below 60%, run a Focus Training session on it before your next mixed drill.

⚙️ Settings & Personalization

What You Can Control

  • 🔓 Theme: switch to the Playful Professional theme for a cleaner alternate look
  • Dark Mode: easy on the eyes for late-night practice sessions
  • Difficulty: Easy or Medium for free; 🔓 Hard unlocks the toughest question variants
  • 🔓 Skip Questions: add a Skip button to drills — move past stuck moments without breaking momentum
  • Sound & Vibration: toggle audio and haptic feedback to match your environment
  • Reduced Motion: turn off animations for distraction-free or accessibility use
  • Daily Goal: set your question target per day (🔓 goals above 20 require Premium)
  • Notifications: daily push reminders so your streak stays intact
  • 🔓 Focus Timer: add time pressure to Focus Training sessions and simulate real exam pacing

📲 Offline & PWA Installation

Install QuantReflex on Your Device

QuantReflex is a Progressive Web App: install it from your browser and use it offline.

  • Android / Chrome: tap the install banner or use menu → "Add to Home Screen"
  • iOS / Safari: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"

Once installed, the app launches full-screen and works offline. Refresh after updates if needed.

💡 Installing gives you a faster, distraction-free training experience.

🛠 Troubleshooting

  • Numpad not showing? Tap the answer input field to bring it back up.
  • Notifications not arriving? Check browser notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Progress not syncing? Ensure you're logged in with an active internet connection.
  • App feels slow after update? Pull down to refresh, or go to Settings → Update App.
  • Streak not updating? Complete at least one session per day. Check your device date/time.

💡 About

QuantReflex

What is QuantReflex?

QuantReflex is a performance-focused quantitative aptitude training platform built for competitive exam preparation. It combines speed drills, concept reinforcement, and mistake-led revision - so you improve both pace and accuracy with measurable consistency.

Unlike passive study apps, QuantReflex is action-first: every session is deliberate practice that directly reflects exam conditions.

Who Is It For?

  • CAT / CET / GMAT aspirants: Build the calculation speed required for QA sections
  • School students (Class 6–12): Master mental math fundamentals for board exams and daily classwork
  • NTSE / Olympiad learners: Sharpen speed and accuracy for competitive math challenges
  • Banking & SSC exam students: Sharpen arithmetic, percentages, and data interpretation reflexes
  • Anyone rebuilding quant fundamentals: Structured daily practice that scales from beginner to advanced

Why QuantReflex?

  • Reflex-first training: Modes simulate real time pressure so you build solving speed, not just knowledge
  • Precision practice: Category-focused drills and mistake-driven review target your exact weak points
  • Measurable progress: Analytics, streaks, and goals convert daily effort into visible, trackable growth
  • Anytime readiness: Installable PWA works offline, so you can train anywhere without excuses
  • Personalized experience: Difficulty, themes, goals, notifications - tuned to how you train best

✨ Core Features

  • 5 practice modes: Quick Drill, Reflex Drill, Timed Test, Focus Training, Review Mistakes
  • 10+ question categories: Squares, Cubes, Percentages, Multiplication, Fractions, Averages, Ratios, Profit & Loss, TSD, Time & Work
  • Learn Vault with formulas, shortcut tricks, tables, and custom notes
  • Analytics dashboard with accuracy, speed, streaks, and category breakdowns
  • Daily goals, push notifications, and streak tracking
  • Two themes (Classic Blue, Playful Professional) with full dark mode
  • Installable offline PWA - no app store required
